About Us Wedana

PT WEDANA LESTARI GROUP is a company engaged in the export industry of tea commodities from Indonesia with a commitment to providing innovative and high-quality tea product solutions. Established in 2022, we are dedicated to providing premium tea that is committed to connecting the best quality from Indonesian tea gardens with the global market.

Why Us?

Several reasons why you should choose us:

Unsurpassed Quality

Indonesian tea is known worldwide for its exceptional quality and taste. Our products set the highest standards.

Sustainable Supply

Indonesia's tropical climate and vast agricultural lands ensure a consistent and sustainable supply of tea throughout the year to meet international market demands.

Competitive Pricing

Benefit from a competitive pricing structure that maximizes your margins and ensures cost effectiveness in your supply chain.

Commitment to Quality Assurance

Our rigorous quality assurance practices ensure that you receive tea products of the highest standard, meeting international quality benchmarks.
